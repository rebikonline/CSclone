ABOUT

CS•BROWSER v4.0

Fully browser-based tactical FPS. Vanilla JS DDA raycasting — no WebGL, no libraries.


ENGINE FEATURES

  • DDA raycasting with per-pixel textures
  • Textured floor & ceiling projection
  • Sprite rendering with Z-buffer
  • Procedural weapon view models with bob & recoil
  • Firebase realtime multiplayer sync
  • Bot AI: vision, reaction time, strafe
  • 15-second buy phase per round
  • Anti-cheat: speed & ammo validation
  • 4 modes: Bomb, DM, CTF, Arms Race
